on starting over

There's something about a blank page that feels both terrifying and freeing at the same time. I've been thinking a lot about what it means to start something new. Not because the old thing was broken, but because you outgrew it.

I built this site because I wanted a quiet corner of the internet. Not to perform, not to brand, not to optimize. Just to put things down. Photos I want to remember. Words I want to keep.

I think we all need a place like that. Somewhere that doesn't ask anything of you except to show up and be honest.

So here it is. My little corner. Welcome.
